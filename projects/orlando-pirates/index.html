02 · Context & Problem

The Gap in the Narrative

Business Context

Orlando Pirates are one of South Africa's most commercially significant sports clubs. Every coaching appointment, cup campaign, and continental run generates public scrutiny — but most analysis stays at the surface, focusing on individual matches or single-season headlines.

Without a long-term view, it is almost impossible to distinguish a temporary slump from a structural problem, or to fairly evaluate whether a manager improved the club or simply inherited momentum.

This matters first within football, where club leadership, coaching continuity, and squad planning are often judged through short-term results rather than longer performance patterns.

"One trophy does not define a football club, and one poor stretch does not fully explain a multi-year cycle. The broader pattern matters more than the loudest moment."

Problem Statement

There is rarely a single interactive view connecting Orlando Pirates' long-term results, leadership-era performance, and goal trends in one place, which makes multi-season comparison harder than it should be.

Specific gaps this project addresses:

  • → No unified view of how win rates shifted across a full decade and multiple coaches
  • → Coaching impact rarely measured beyond trophy counts — the numbers tell a different story
  • → Goal output and defensive trends were never visualised side-by-side across full seasons
  • → Individual player contributions were disconnected from team-level performance context

07 · Findings

Key Insights

Leadership Continuity

Unstable Leadership Directly Hurts Results

During the 2019–20 and 2022–23 seasons — both marked by interim management and changing direction — win rates dropped below 50% and goal output weakened. The pattern suggests that instability coincided with poorer performance in these periods, even if the page does not prove leadership uncertainty was the only cause.

Sustained Investment

Long-Term Appointments Produce the Best Returns

The Sredojevic (2017–19) and Riveiro (2023–26) eras — both built on clearer continuity — produced the club's highest win rates and strongest goal output in this view. That does not prove continuity alone drove the outcome, but it does suggest the club looked more stable when coaching direction held over time.

Structural Imbalance

Attacking Strength Repeatedly Masked Defensive Risk

Across multiple seasons, goal output improved while defensive metrics remained inconsistent — meaning positive results often depended on outscoring opponents rather than controlling matches. The data helps show when that attacking-versus-defensive imbalance became a vulnerability.

Resource Allocation

Squad Depth Is a Strategic Constraint, Not a Minor Detail

In seasons where CAF continental fixtures created congestion, PSL form appears to have dipped, which may point to a squad-depth constraint rather than a purely motivational issue. The page supports that concern directionally, even if it does not isolate congestion as the only reason for weaker league form.

09 · Process & Deliverables

How It Was Built

Approach

The project was built in three phases: organising the data, designing the visuals, and adding the narrative layer that connects numbers to meaning. Each section is meant to answer a specific question without forcing the reader to decode raw match tables first.

Chart types were matched to each metric: line charts for trends over time, bar charts for era comparisons, a donut for aggregate distribution, and area fills for cumulative measures like goal difference.

The layout is designed to work for experienced analysts who want to scan quickly and for business stakeholders who want the headline conclusions without the footnotes.

HTML / CSS JavaScript (ES6) Chart.js 4 Google Fonts Public match records

Deliverables

01

Interactive Performance Dashboard

A single-page HTML dashboard that consolidates win rates, goal trends, and era breakdowns — built with Chart.js and deployable without a backend or proprietary tools.

02

Coaching Era Comparison Module

A visual and tabular breakdown of all major coaching periods — measuring impact in win rate, goals, and phase classification rather than relying on trophy counts alone.

03

Narrative Insight Layer

Data-led insight cards that translate visual patterns into plain-language conclusions — making the analysis readable for decision-makers, not just data specialists.
