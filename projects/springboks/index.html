Springbok10-Year Arc

I built this data storytelling project to visualise the Springboks' decade-long growth, from the difficult 2016 period through the Erasmus rebuild, back-to-back World Cups, and the 2025 high point. Verified data is used where available, and estimates are clearly flagged.

Estimated ESTYear-by-year cap distributions are estimated from career totals plus known debut years. Era try splits are estimated from career totals, debut years, and known tournament data. Win-rate-when-starting is derived from public squad and result data rather than an official published source.
What this is notThis is not a GOAT ranking. There are no composite scores or weighted rankings, and players in different positions are not compared directly. The goal is to show individual growth arcs inside the wider team story.
Several figures, especially year-by-year cap splits and era try portions, are estimates derived from career totals and debut-year data. They are directionally reliable but not officially published by SA Rugby, and each estimate is flagged for that reason.
